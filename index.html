Our Services

We provide specialized physiotherapy services designed to treat a wide range of conditions and enhance your overall well-being.

Orthopedic Conditions

Treatment and rehabilitation for bone, joint, and ligament injuries, including post-fracture care, joint replacements, and arthritis management.

Neuro Rehabilitation

Tailored programs to restore mobility and independence in patients with stroke, spinal cord injuries, Parkinson's, and other neurological disorders.

Sports Rehabilitation

Injury recovery and performance enhancement plans for athletes to ensure safe return to sport and prevent re-injury.

Musculoskeletal Correction

Corrective therapies for muscle imbalances, joint stiffness, and post-injury recovery to restore optimal body mechanics.

Women’s Health

Specialized physiotherapy for prenatal, postnatal, pelvic floor rehabilitation, and other women-specific health concerns.

Postural Correction

Assessment and treatment of poor posture to reduce pain, improve alignment, and prevent long-term musculoskeletal problems.

Pain Management

Evidence-based therapies to relieve acute and chronic pain through exercises, manual therapy, and advanced modalities.

Therapeutic Exercises

Customized exercise programs to restore mobility, strength, and endurance while aiding in recovery and injury prevention.

Neonatal Care

Gentle and safe physiotherapy programs for newborns to address conditions like torticollis, delayed milestones, and motor skill development.
